Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect your VIT email ID, registration number, and contact details to verify your student status. For sellers, we collect product information and payment details.

2. How We Use Information

  • Verify student identity through VIT credentials
  • Facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers
  • Provide customer support for marketplace activities
  • Send important notifications about your transactions
  • Improve our marketplace services for VIT community

3. Data Sharing Within VIT

We only share necessary transaction information between verified VIT students participating in a transaction. Your personal contact information is never shared without consent.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures suitable for student transactions including encryption of sensitive data and secure authentication through VIT credentials.

5. Your Rights as VIT Student

You can request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us through your VIT email. You can manage your listings and account preferences anytime.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to better serve the VIT community. Significant changes will be notified through your VIT email address.

Effective Date: March 2025

For any privacy concerns, please contact our support team from your VIT email.
